#Arcade game frogger components:
The Game consists of three areas grass, pavement and water. Player starts the game stands on the grass and try to cross the pavement to reach the water so win but there are some enemies who crosses the road and the player have to avoid them because if he collide with one of them he returned back to the star point (grass).

#How to play the game & Instructions:
First the player can control the character by using the arrow keys (up, right, down and left. The player will find some enemies (bugs) crosses the road once the enemy get out of the screen it will reappear again and also the enemies don’t have constant speed their speed randomly each time they appear. So take care! .And to play the game you have to download the game folder and open index.html in the browser of your choice.
